Data Control Center

Account & Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Google Play Store & Apple App Store developer regulations

At ArrowLogicx, we respect your data privacy and control rights. In accordance with Google Play's Account Deletion Policy, if you have registered an account on our Instant Maid App, you may request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data using this secure portal.

What data will be permanently deleted?
  • Profile registration data (name, email, phone number, credentials).
  • Stored address coordinates and matching preferences.
  • Payment token records and service histories.
  • For Service Partners: Uploaded police verification logs, KYC checks, and reference records.

Note: Active subscriptions, pending payouts, or uncompleted matching service transactions must be resolved prior to account deletion.
