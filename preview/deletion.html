Account & Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Google Play Store & Apple App Store developer regulations

At Zafabit, we respect your data privacy. If you have registered an account on our Zafabit Instant House Help App, you may request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data using this form.

What data will be permanently deleted?
  • Profile registration data (name, email, phone number, credentials).
  • Stored address coordinates and matching preferences.
  • Payment token records and service histories.
  • For Service Partners: Uploaded police verification logs, KYC checks, and reference records.

Note: Active subscriptions, pending payouts, or uncompleted transactions must be resolved prior to account deletion.
